What is an ADU?

An Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) is a fully self-contained home built on your existing property. It includes a kitchen, bathroom, living space, and private entry—so family members or tenants can live independently while staying close.

Why homeowners build ADUs

Generate Income

Turn unused yard space into a revenue-generating rental unit.

Keep Family Close

Support multigenerational living with independence and dignity.

Flexible Space

Create a home office, guest suite, or long-term investment.
